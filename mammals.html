Mammals

Dog

Loyal and friendly companions, dogs are one of the most common mammalian pets.

Cat

Cats are independent and affectionate pets loved for their playful nature.

Rabbit

Rabbits are gentle pets that enjoy social interaction and cuddles.

Guinea Pig

Guinea pigs are social, gentle rodents that thrive in small groups.

Hamster

Hamsters are small nocturnal pets known for storing food in their cheeks.

Hedgehog

These spiky little pets are shy but can be quite affectionate with care and patience.
